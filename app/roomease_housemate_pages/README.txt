RoomEase - Housemate Pages
==========================

Files included:
- find_housemate.html
- preferences.html
- matches.html
- profile.html
- chat.html
- roommate_card_component.html
- assets/js/main.js (light helper)

How to merge:
- Copy these files into your existing RoomEase project folder.
- They expect your stylesheet at: assets/css/style.css (keep your existing file).
- If your main.js is different, you can replace assets/js/main.js or merge logic.
- All image placeholders use https://placehold.co/ and will load online.
